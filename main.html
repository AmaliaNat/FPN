WELCOME TO FPN UITM MELAKA
The Faculty of Accounting is one of the earliest faculties in UiTM with the establishment of the RIDA (Rural Industrial Development Authority) Training Hall in 1956. As a government agency, this faculty has the role of upgrading Bumiputera social status by providing training and educational opportunities. The first program offered was in 1956 the London Chambers of Commerce and Industry (LCCI), a UK accounting program followed by the Australian Society of Accountants program. The main goal of this faculty is to produce semi-professionals in the field of accounting.
