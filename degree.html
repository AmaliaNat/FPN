Bachelor of Accountancy (Hons.) | AC220
The Bachelor of Accountancy (Hons.) programme was introduced in 1981. It has been designed with the objective of providing accounting education at professional level to meet the demands of both the public and private sectors. The duration of this programme is 4 years (8 semesters) for graduates from STPM/Matriculation or any equivalent qualification. Students who have completed Diploma in Accountancy, UiTM will begin the programme at semester three (3). Other Diploma candidates may also apply for credit transfer if they fulfilled UiTM transfer credit requirements.
